# Supporting Data

This folder contains the supplementary datasets associated with the manuscript:

**Repurposing Nucleos(t)ide Analogs as Purine Nucleoside Phosphorylase Inhibitors through Gaussian Accelerated Molecular Dynamics Simulations**

## Contents

### Per-residue MM-PBSA decomposition

- Table_S1_C0_PerResidue_MM_PBSA.xlsx
  - Native ligand (Immucillin-H)

- Table_S2_C1_PerResidue_MM_PBSA.xlsx
  - Flavin adenine dinucleotide

- Table_S3_C2_PerResidue_MM_PBSA.xlsx
  - 6-Hydroxy-FAD

- Table_S4_C3_PerResidue_MM_PBSA.xlsx
  - Evodenoson

- Table_S5_C4_PerResidue_MM_PBSA.xlsx
  - 2-Ethylthiomethylene-N6-isopentenyl-adenosine-5′-monophosphate

## Additional files

- Docking_Grid_Coordinates.xlsx
- Virtual_Screening_Results.xlsx

The MM-PBSA decomposition tables contain residue-wise van der Waals, electrostatic, polar solvation, non-polar solvation, total binding free energy contributions, standard deviation (SD), and standard error of the mean (SEM) calculated from 400 snapshots extracted from the final 20 ns of the 500 ns Gaussian accelerated molecular dynamics (GaMD) simulations.
